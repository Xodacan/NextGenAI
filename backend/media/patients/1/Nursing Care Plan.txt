Paediatric Ward Care Plan – Night Shift
Date: 27-Aug-2025
Goals:
Maintain SpO2 ≥ 94 % RA.
Relieve respiratory distress.
Reduce anxiety for child & family.
Interventions:
Continuous SpO₂, HR, RR.
Nebulisers q2h – document response.
Encourage oral fluids (avoid over-hydration).
Distraction play & parent presence.
Educate parents on spacer technique bedside.
